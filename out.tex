\begin{frame}
    \frametitle{Output}
    Cyclus records all transactions between \texttt{Facilities}
    and other metrics unique to each archetype, such as:
    \begin{itemize}

        \item Cycamore::Enrichment - SWU per timestep
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
